# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: February 11, 2026

## 1. Information Collection

### Automated Data Collection
Our service operates through automated research and publishing systems that collect publicly available information from:

- Academic research databases (ArXiv)
- Patent filing systems (USPTO)
- Regulatory document databases (SEC)
- Market data APIs and public sources

### User Interaction Data
When users interact with our published content on social media platforms:
- Interaction data is governed by the respective platform's privacy policy
- We do not directly collect personal information from social media interactions
- Public comments and engagements are subject to platform terms

### No Personal Data Collection
We do not:
- Collect personal information from users
- Track individual user behavior
- Store user contact information
- Require account registration
- Use cookies or tracking technologies

## 2. Data Usage

### Research and Analysis
Collected public information is used solely for:
- Educational content creation
- Market trend analysis
- Academic research compilation
- Investment signal identification

### Automated Publishing
Information is processed through automated systems to:
- Generate educational content summaries
- Create structured analysis reports
- Publish research insights on social platforms
- Provide market intelligence updates

## 3. Data Sharing

### No Personal Data Sharing
Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to share with third parties.

### Public Information Sharing
Our analysis and insights, derived from public sources, are shared through:
- Social media platforms (X, etc.)
- Public research repositories
- Educational content channels

### Third-Party Services
We may use third-party services for:
- Content hosting (GitHub)
- Social media publishing (X API)
- Market data access (public APIs)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

## 4. Data Security

### Public Data Nature
As we work exclusively with publicly available information, traditional data security concerns regarding personal information do not apply.

### System Security
We implement appropriate technical measures to secure our automated systems and prevent unauthorized access or manipulation.

## 5. Data Retention

### Research Data
Public research data is retained as needed for ongoing analysis and historical reference. This includes academic papers, patent information, and regulatory filings that are publicly available.

### No Personal Data Retention
Since no personal data is collected, there are no personal data retention considerations.

## 6. User Rights

### Access to Public Information
All source materials we analyze are publicly available. Users can access original sources directly through their respective databases.

### Content Control
Users control their interaction with our published content through social media platform controls and settings.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for professional and educational use by adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 and our content is not directed toward children.

## 8. International Users

Our service processes publicly available information that may originate from various jurisdictions. Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with local privacy laws.

## 9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

## 10. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions, please contact via GitHub issues on this repository.
